01 — About the Study

Research Purpose

This study is part of a master’s thesis research project that examines how a serious game enhanced with narrative and user experience design affects environmental sustainability awareness and learning experience.

You are invited to participate in this study by playing a digital game and completing short questionnaires about your experience. Participation is completely voluntary.

If you agree to participate, you will:

  • play a browser-based digital game for approximately 30–40 minutes,
  • complete a short questionnaires before and after gameplay

The total participation time is expected to be approximately 35–45 minutes.

The study involves minimal risk. Some participants may experience mild fatigue or slight discomfort associated with playing a digital game or completing questionnaires. You may stop participating at any time without providing a reason and without any negative consequences.

All collected data will be anonymized. The data will be used only for academic and research purposes, including the completion of a master’s thesis and possible scientific publications or presentations. Your identity will not be revealed in any report, publication, or presentation related to this research.

Your participation in this study is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty or disadvantage. If you choose to withdraw, any identifiable data related to you will be removed upon request.
